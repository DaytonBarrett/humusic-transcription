WHAT IT DOES

Built for musicians,
by a musician.

Every feature designed to remove the barriers between hearing music and writing it down.

Audio Recording

Record directly in the app or upload existing files — MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A, and more are all supported out of the box.

AI Transcription

Cutting-edge pitch detection and rhythm analysis converts your audio into accurate musical notation automatically.

Sheet Music Generation

Watch your audio transform into properly notated sheet music — treble clef, bass clef, grand staff, or lead sheet.

Flexible Export

Export as PDF for printing, MusicXML for notation software like Sibelius and Finale, or MIDI for DAWs and production.

In-App Editing

Fine-tune notes, correct octaves, adjust rhythms, add dynamics and articulations — all without leaving humusic.

Multi-Instrument Support

Piano, guitar, violin, voice — humusic handles melodic instruments and even multi-voice polyphonic recordings.

THE PROCESS

Three steps. One score.

From raw audio to print-ready notation in under a minute.

Listening...
01

Record or Upload

Use the built-in microphone recorder or drag and drop any audio file. humusic accepts all major formats — no conversion required. Hit record and play your melody, or upload a studio recording. Both work equally well.

  • MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A, OGG
  • Built-in mic or external interface
  • Up to 30-minute recordings

AI Analysis

humusic's transcription engine analyzes every millisecond of your audio — detecting pitch, rhythm, dynamics, and timing simultaneously. The AI identifies individual notes even in dense chords and complex passages.

  • Pitch detection to the cent
  • Tempo and time signature inference
  • Polyphonic chord recognition

THE STORY

Music should be
accessible to everyone.

humusic was born from a simple frustration: great musical ideas disappear because most people can't write them down. Notation is a skill that takes years to learn — and that's a problem. Whether you're a trained musician with decades of experience or someone who hums tunes in the shower, your music deserves to exist on paper.

I'm building humusic to be the bridge between hearing music and writing it — making professional-quality notation accessible to every musician, at every level.
